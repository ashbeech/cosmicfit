Cultivate a cool detachment when evaluating a garment. Look past the label and trust the physical weight to tell you the truth.

Your exact operating principle is clean geometry + softness + one polished signal. When getting dressed, assemble the look fast and do not overthink it. Your ultimate test is a simple somatic response.
