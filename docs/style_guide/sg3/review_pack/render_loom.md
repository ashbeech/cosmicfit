## 3. The Textures

_Train your hands to filter your wardrobe before your eyes even register the garment._

Your wardrobe relies on honest fabric to build balanced silhouettes. Trust your hands over the mirror. Execute the edge test on every prospective purchase. Grip the hem tightly between your fingers. If the textile feels spongy or pushes back against your pressure, leave it on the hanger. You need pliable materials to construct your weightless layers. Seek Crisp cotton poplin for shirting that establishes clean architectural boundaries. Rely on Fine ribbed merino for close base layers that guarantee sharp, easy movement. Pick up Worsted wool for trousers that drape cleanly and resist creasing through a long day. Finish with Technical nylon for mobile outerwear that blocks cool air while weighing little. Stock your wardrobe from the list below.

- **Crisp cotton poplin** (rank 1): shirting with clean architectural boundaries and pure separation
